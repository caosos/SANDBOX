# Senior Mobility Repair

## Purpose

Build a practical Conway / Faulkner County service around small mobility-equipment repairs and senior safety help.

## Core offer

Mobile small repairs and accessories for seniors and families:

- Walker and rollator wheels
- Rollator brake adjustments
- Bearings and loose parts
- Cane tips and walker skis
- Manual wheelchair arm pads
- Cup holders, mirrors, bags, pouches, and accessories
- Basic battery swaps where appropriate
- Small home comfort/safety fixes that fall through the cracks

## Not the lane

This is not licensed medical care, clinical assessment, or major powered mobility repair unless properly trained/insured and clearly inside the legal/safety lane.

Avoid structural repairs or powered equipment work that could create injury risk unless a qualified partner handles it.

## Lead sources

Primary targets are the people who spend time with seniors and see problems early:

- Executive Directors / Administrators
- Resident Care Directors / Wellness Directors / DONs
- Activities Directors / Life Enrichment Directors
- Transportation Directors / drivers
- Maintenance Directors
- CNAs, caregivers, med techs, home health aides
- Physical therapists and occupational therapists
- Rehab directors
- Hospice social workers
- Home-care agency managers and care coordinators
- DME / medical supply store managers

## Clean pitch

I help seniors and families keep mobility equipment working and handle the small safety fixes nobody wants to come out for.

## Boundary

Do not use Brookdale residents, families, coworkers, company time, company tools, or the Brookdale position to build this service. Build it outside that wall.
