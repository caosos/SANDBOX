## Pricing language

Simple pricing:

- Free quote by photo/message when possible
- Small mobile visit fee when a trip is required
- Labor + parts for repairs
- No pressure and no surprise work
- If it is outside my lane, I will say so

Avoid advertising a paid inspection as the main offer. Sell the repair/help, not the check.
