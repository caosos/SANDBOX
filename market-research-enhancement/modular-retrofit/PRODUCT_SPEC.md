# Product Spec: Modular Retrofit Insert System

## Core Concept

A retrofit system that converts fixed-opening full-size silicone/TPE dolls into a removable, cleanable, replaceable modular insert system.

The product is not just a soft insert. The real product is the reinforced retrofit collar/socket system that lets the insert work without destroying the doll body.

## System Components

1. Reinforced retrofit collar/socket
2. Removable insert module
3. Cleaning and drying kit
4. Optional low-voltage powered accessory pack
5. Installation guide, assessment process, or controlled installation service

## Main Customer Problem

Owners may discover after purchase that fixed openings are harder to clean, harder to dry, harder to repair, and less serviceable than removable-insert designs.

If the doll was expensive, replacement may be less attractive than repair or retrofit.

## MVP 1: Passive Retrofit Socket + Removable Insert

No motor. No heat. No electronics.

Purpose: prove the mechanical system first.

Includes:

- reinforced retrofit collar
- removable soft insert
- rear flange or pull-tab system
- cleaning/drying tool
- installation template
- compatible adhesive/reinforcement material
- material compatibility test swatches

Must solve:

- clean removal
- repeatable insertion
- edge reinforcement
- tear prevention
- moisture access
- insert replacement
- standardized fit

## MVP 2: Powered Accessory Version

After the passive version works, add low-voltage power.

Recommended architecture:

- wall outlet
- UL-listed 5V DC power supply
- inline fuse/current limiting
- external control box
- waterproof quick-disconnect connector
- removable powered insert module

Rules:

- no 120V AC inside the product
- no buried coin-cell battery
- no sealed lithium battery inside early prototypes
- no heat without sensor and cutoff
- powered components must disconnect before cleaning

Possible features:

- low-voltage warming element
- vibration motor
- temperature sensor
- thermal cutoff
- manual speed/temperature controller

## Retrofit Collar

The collar is the main invention.

Purpose:

- reinforce the cut edge
- prevent tear propagation
- create a standard socket
- hold the insert under realistic handling loads
- protect doll material from repeated stress
- improve cleaning access

Possible designs:

- soft silicone collar with flange
- TPE-compatible collar
- two-part compression collar
- adhesive-bonded collar
- mechanical-lock collar
- hybrid collar with flexible reinforcement mesh

Design rules:

- rounded openings only
- no sharp corners
- no thin unsupported edge
- no hidden absorbent material
- no cavity that traps fluid permanently

## Removable Insert Module

First version should be passive and simple.

Features:

- soft cast body-contact material
- tapered outside geometry
- rear flange for removal
- smooth exterior
- washable
- replaceable
- compatible with the collar/socket

The insert should not rely on friction alone. It needs a flange, keyed shape, lip, or socket retention geometry.

## Cleaning/Drying Kit

Include from day one.

Kit may include:

- rinse bottle or pump
- drying wand
- absorbent drying sleeves
- storage case
- compatible care product
- care card
- replacement schedule

This may become the first sellable product before the full retrofit kit.

## Installation Model

Best early model:

1. Owner submits photos, measurements, material type, and doll model if known.
2. Compatibility is rated low, medium, or high risk.
3. First installations are controlled, not pure DIY.
4. Failures are documented.
5. DIY kit is released only after the install process is proven.

Reason: DIY customers may cut wrong, tear the doll, create an unsafe condition, or blame the kit.

## First Sellable Products

1. Cleaning/drying kit
2. Replacement insert line for dolls already designed for removable inserts
3. Retrofit assessment service
4. Passive retrofit socket kit
5. Controlled installation service
6. Optional 5V powered premium insert

- retrofit assessment: $25–$75

Do not lock pricing until customer testing.

## Defensible Moat

The insert itself is not the moat.

The moat is:

- standardized retrofit collar
- material compatibility testing
- installation templates
- reinforcement method
- compatibility database
- repair knowledge
- replacement ecosystem
- trust in a private, high-risk product category

Build a compatibility database by:

- brand
- model
- material
- fixed/removable configuration
- wall thickness
- skeleton interference
- successful retrofit yes/no
- recommended collar size
- adhesive/reinforcement type
- known failure points

## Safety and Liability Rules

Hard rules:

- no mains voltage inside product
- no unprotected internal battery in early prototypes
- no heat without sensor and cutoff
- no sharp internal edges
- no absorbent hidden material
- no claim of universal compatibility
- no customer modification without written consent and risk disclosure
- no medical claims
- no body-safe claim unless material documentation supports it
- no warranty promise on the original doll after modification

Required warnings:

- modification may void doll warranty
- cutting TPE/silicone can permanently damage the doll
- not all dolls are compatible
- powered accessories must be disconnected before cleaning
- inspect for tears before use
- discontinue use if material breaks down
- clean and dry after use

## Working Verdict

The business is not a generic adult accessory. The business is an aftermarket modular conversion system for expensive full-size dolls that were originally built with fixed anatomy.

The mold makes the part. The collar makes the business.
