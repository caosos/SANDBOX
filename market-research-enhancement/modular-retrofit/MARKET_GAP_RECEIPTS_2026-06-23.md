# Market Gap Receipt Notes — 2026-06-23

## Question

Find one existing product that already does the proposed concept:

> aftermarket fixed-opening doll retrofit plug/collar/socket that converts a fixed silicone/TPE doll opening into a removable insert system.

## Fast Findings

### Found: standalone sleeve category

Fleshlight is an existing artificial-vagina sleeve/product category. Search results summarize that the inner sleeve is available with different openings and internal textures.

Source:
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fleshlight

Use case for this project:
Reference for sleeve category, materials, textures, and consumer expectations. Not proof of doll retrofit.

### Found: doll replacement insert

Coeros sells WM Sex Doll TPE/Silicone Removable Vagina Insert for about $29.50–$34.50.

Key source quote:
"This vaginal insert is exclusively compatible with dolls featuring a removable vaginal option, not those with a fixed vaginal configuration."

Source:
https://www.coeros.com/product/wm-vagina-insert/

Use case for this project:
Strong evidence that replacement inserts exist, but they are not solving fixed-opening retrofit.

### Found: factory removable-vs-fixed design explanation

YouQDOLL explains fixed vs insert designs.

Key source quote:
"The fixed vagina is a built-in-vagina, permanently molded into the doll’s body." It also says the insert option has a slightly wider internal opening to accommodate a removable sleeve and is easier to clean.

Source:
https://youqdoll.com/pages/common-options

Use case for this project:
Strong evidence that factory removable designs require body geometry designed for removable sleeves.

### Found: premium removable/powered insert ecosystem

RealDoll sells AI Vagina / electronic insert for Realdoll.

Source:
https://www.realdoll.com/product/ai-vagina/

Use case for this project:
Shows premium powered/removable insert market exists, but this is not a universal fixed-opening retrofit kit.

### Not found in current searches

No clear evidence found yet of a widely available aftermarket kit or service that converts fixed-opening full-size silicone/TPE dolls into removable insert systems using a retrofit collar/socket/plug.

## Working Verdict

Existing products solve:

- standalone sleeves
- factory removable insert systems
- replacement inserts for already-removable dolls
- premium powered inserts for compatible premium dolls

Current white space remains:

- aftermarket fixed-opening retrofit collar/socket/plug system
- controlled conversion service
- compatibility kit/database
- reinforced socket that makes fixed dolls modular

## Business Implication

The opportunity is not simply "make a sleeve."

The opportunity is:

> make non-modular dolls modular.

That is the part not clearly found in the market scan so far.
