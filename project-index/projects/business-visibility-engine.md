# Business Launch and Visibility Engine

Status: active concept/product lane.

## Core thesis

An AI-assisted business launch and visibility system that audits, sets up, cleans up, and maintains a business footprint across websites, listings, reviews, maps, resources, classes, and local partner networks.

## Product direction

- Start small with one simple offer: scan business footprint, fix what is missing, keep it updated monthly.
- Use red/green reporting: what is good, what is bad, what can be fixed, what the customer can do.
- Local-first resource routing by state/city.
- Business 101 Zoom classes.
- Partner/referral network: banks, SBA/SBDC/SCORE-type resources, chambers, attorneys, CPAs, local services.
- Monthly enhancement agents maintain accuracy/freshness.
